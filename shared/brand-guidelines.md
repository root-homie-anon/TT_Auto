# Brand Guidelines — Health is Wealth

## Tone
- Confident, direct, results-focused
- NOT salesy, NOT aggressive, NOT fake-urgent
- Speak like a knowledgeable friend sharing a find, not a pitchman

## Content Rules
- Always lead with the problem, not the product
- No "limited time!!!" or "buy now before it's gone"
- Let the product and results speak for themselves
- Use specific numbers when possible (e.g., "4.8 stars from 2,000+ reviews")
- No medical claims: never say "cures", "treats", or "prevents" — use "supports", "helps", "promotes"

## Video Structure
- Hook (0-3s): Problem statement or result tease
- Context (3-8s): Why this matters / who it's for
- Product reveal (8-15s): Show the product, key features
- Social proof (15-20s): Reviews, ratings, sold count
- CTA (last 3-5s): "Link in bio" or "Find it on TikTok Shop"

## Visual Style
- Clean, modern, health-forward aesthetic
- White/green/earth tone palette
- Text: large, bold, readable on mobile in first 2 seconds
- No cluttered overlays — max 2 text elements on screen at once

## Caption Format
- Product name first line
- 1-2 sentence value prop
- "Link in bio" or shop CTA
- Hashtags: #tiktokshop #healthiswealth #[category] #healthfinds #tiktokmademebuyit #wellness

## Duration Targets
- Feed videos: 15-30 seconds
- Product demos: 45-60 seconds
- Default: 20-25 seconds
