# Product Categories — Health is Wealth

## supplements
Vitamins, minerals, protein powders, herbal supplements, probiotics, collagen, sea moss, turmeric.
- Default format: voiceover
- Duration: 20s
- Rationale: Claims need voiceover context, no demo possible
- Focus: benefits, ingredients, reviews

## fitness-tools
Exercise equipment, resistance bands, yoga mats, ab rollers, dumbbells, pull-up bars.
- Default format: demo
- Duration: 30s
- Rationale: Visual transformation drives CTR
- Focus: show it in use, before/after feel

## recovery
Massage guns, foam rollers, ice packs, compression sleeves, TENS units, posture correctors.
- Default format: demo
- Duration: 30s
- Rationale: Show the sensation, lead with pain point
- Focus: demonstrate relief, target specific pain points

## sleep-wellness
Sleep aids, white noise machines, weighted blankets, aromatherapy, blue light glasses, meditation tools.
- Default format: hook-text
- Duration: 20s
- Rationale: Emotional hook, soft sell
- Focus: peaceful aesthetic, transformation from tired to rested

## weight-management
Scales, meal prep containers, portion tools, metabolism supplements, fitness trackers.
- Default format: voiceover-before-after
- Duration: 25s
- Rationale: Results-forward content converts
- Focus: transformation narrative, social proof

## general-health
Air purifiers, water filters, humidifiers, health monitors, first aid, hygiene products.
- Default format: voiceover
- Duration: 20s
- Rationale: Informational approach works best for utility products
- Focus: problem/solution framing, specs that matter
