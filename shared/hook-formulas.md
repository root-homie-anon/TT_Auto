# Hook Formulas — Health is Wealth

## Problem-First Hooks (highest converting)
- "If you struggle with [problem], you need to see this"
- "I wish I knew about this [product type] sooner"
- "Stop wasting money on [inferior solution] — try this instead"
- "The reason your [body part/function] isn't improving"
- "Nobody talks about this [health benefit] hack"

## Results-Forward Hooks
- "This [product] changed my [routine/health metric] in [timeframe]"
- "[Number] people are using this for [benefit] — here's why"
- "The [product type] that's going viral for a reason"
- "I tested [product] for [timeframe] — here's what happened"

## Curiosity Hooks
- "Why is everyone buying this [product type] right now?"
- "The [product] TikTok Shop keeps selling out"
- "You won't believe what this [product type] does for [benefit]"

## Pattern Interrupt Hooks
- "Wait — before you buy another [competitor product]..."
- "Your [routine] is missing this one thing"
- "POV: you finally found a [product type] that actually works"

## Rules
- Hook must be readable on screen within 2 seconds
- Always lead with the problem or result, never the product name
- No fake urgency or countdown language
- Keep under 15 words for on-screen text version
